About Click Marketing

We are a team of digital marketing experts.

Established in 2019, we pride ourselves on being a small but professional client-focused team. We are with you every step of the way, from concept to success.

From your initial consultation, you will see how we stand apart from other organisations. We develop a close, unique working relationship with all our clients that is results and performance-focused.

We don’t work for you, we work with you.

At Click Marketing, we understand that every client’s business and requirements are different. That is why we offer a bespoke personalised service so that your business can become established, succeed and grow online.

Click Marketing supports small to medium-sized businesses to improve their web, online and social media presence and performance. Our mission is to cultivate your brand and grow through digital strategies and creative campaigns.

We will help you develop a winning digital marketing strategy that works for you, your brand and your business. Click Marketing primarily works with UK-based start-ups and small to medium businesses, but our expertise and success have seen our services used globally.

Would you like to see how we can help your business? Please feel free to book a consultation with our Director, Amy, to see just what Click Marketing can do for you.

We would love to work with a business just like yours.

Meet Amy

Amy is the founder and director of Click Marketing. She has built one of the UK’s fastest-growing digital marketing agencies and gets results for clients where other agencies struggle.
